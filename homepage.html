The UA Mission:

We train the world's next heroes. Our program teaches kids to be heroes. To wear their own hero uniform with pride! For to fight villains there must be heroes. We teach to fight, to think and to be kind! Every hero is a Quirk user, but not every Quirk user is a hero! Our program find those who have the power, determination and the mind to do the amazing hero work. We do everything over the top. We go plus ULTRA.

The Hero Program

Have a cool quirk? Blast explosions from your hands? Super strength? Then your place may be in the Hero Program of UA. Through camps, simulations, controlled fights with professors and other real-life based exercises at the UA we make sure all of our student use their quirks to the fullest!
